| Concern | Mitigation |
|---------|------------|
| MLM / Pyramid | No recruitment incentives, flat commission |
| Securities | Software license + revenue share, not investment |
| Passive Income Promise | Tied to platform growth, not guaranteed |

| Use | Avoid |
