## 3. The "Partner Traffic" Bonus (Super-Affiliate Program)

### How It Works

1. **Auto-Upgrade:** All Founding Members automatically upgraded to Super-Affiliate status
2. **Traffic Rotator Injection:** Affiliate link injected into global "Traffic Rotator" pool
3. **Organic Traffic Share:** When AgentWorks runs ads or ranks on Google, a portion of organic traffic is redirected through founder affiliate links
4. **Passive Commission:** Founders earn 30-40% commissions on any conversions from rotator traffic

### Affiliate Tier Progression

| Tier | Referral Threshold | Commission Rate |
|------|-------------------|-----------------|
| Standard | 0+ referrals | 30% |
| Silver | 10+ referrals | 35% |
| Gold | 25+ referrals | 40% |
| Platinum | 50+ referrals | 50% |

### Example Earnings Scenarios

| Scenario | Calculation | Earnings |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| 5 Silver referrals | 5 × $199 × 0.55* | $548.50 |
| 3 Gold referrals | 3 × $249 × 0.65* | $486.45 |
| 2 Diamond referrals | 2 × $299 × 0.73* | $439.20 |
| 10 mixed referrals | (weighted average) | $1,474.16 |

*Includes base commission + tier bonus

### Traffic Rotator Pool

- **Pool Entry:** Automatic upon founding membership purchase
- **Distribution:** Algorithmic rotation based on affiliate tier and engagement
- **Sources:** Paid ads, organic search traffic, social media campaigns
- **Exclusivity:** Pool closes permanently when 1,000th spot is claimed

## 6. Legal & Compliance Framing

### Utility-First Protection

The primary legal shield is the **utility-first** positioning:

1. **Users pay for SOFTWARE** - A tangible Lifetime Access license
2. **Value delivered immediately** - Full platform access upon purchase
3. **Income is secondary** - If rotator never generates income, they still received the AI development tool
4. **No investment promise** - Not structured as an investment vehicle

### "Bonus" Framing (Marketing Language)

| Do Use | Avoid |
|--------|-------|
| "Partner Traffic Bonus" | "Guaranteed income" |
| "Affiliate Perk" | "Passive investment" |
| "Active Founding Member benefits" | "Returns on investment" |
| "Commission opportunity" | "Profit sharing" |

### Key Language Patterns

- **"Active Founding Member"** - Implies engagement is required
- **"Bonus affiliate status"** - Secondary to software value
- **"Opportunity to earn"** - No guarantees implied
- **"Perk of membership"** - Added benefit, not primary value

### Scarcity Compliance

- 1,000 limit applies to **Lifetime License** (a real product limitation)
- Standard SaaS marketing practice
- No false scarcity - numbers are tracked publicly
- Clear terms of what happens post-sellout
