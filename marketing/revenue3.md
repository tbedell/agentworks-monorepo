## 1. Executive Summary

This model transforms founder compensation from "affiliate marketing" into a **royalty-like structure** where founders earn passive income on ALL platform users through round-robin assignmentâ€”regardless of who referred them.

### The Core Concept: "The 29% CAC Stack"

| Allocation | Recipient | Condition |
|------------|-----------|-----------|
| **25%** | Active Developer Affiliate | If they referred the user |
| **1-4%** | Passive Founder | Always (Round-Robin assigned) |
| **Up to 29%** | Total Marketing CAC | Maximum allocation |

**Key Insight:** If a user comes in **Direct** (no developer referral), the company keeps the 25%, but the Founder **still gets their 1-4%**.

### Why This Matters for Investors

| Old Model | New Model |
|-----------|-----------|
| "Affiliate Marketing" | "Royalty / Dividend" |
| High Commission (30-50%) | Low Commission (1-4%) |
| Low Volume dependent | High Volume dependent |
| Active selling required | Passive ownership |
| MLM concerns | Revenue share structure |

## 3. The Universal Round-Robin Mechanism

### How It Works

1. **Every paid user** that enters AgentWorks is assigned to a Founder slot
2. Assignment is via **Round-Robin** (sequential distribution)
3. The Founder earns their tier % on **that user's subscription for life**
4. Assignment is **permanent** - the user stays with that Founder forever

### Round-Robin Math

| Total Users | Users per Founder | Assignment Cycle |
|-------------|-------------------|------------------|
| 1,000 | 1 each | 1 full rotation |
| 10,000 | 10 each | 10 rotations |
| 100,000 | 100 each | 100 rotations |
| 1,000,000 | 1,000 each | 1,000 rotations |

### Weighted Commission Pool

Since different tiers have different commission rates, the **average commission per user** varies based on round-robin position:

| Tier | Slots | Commission | Weighted Contribution |
|------|-------|------------|----------------------|
| Bronze | 500 | 1% | 500 Ã— 1% = 500 |
| Silver | 300 | 2% | 300 Ã— 2% = 600 |
| Gold | 150 | 3% | 150 Ã— 3% = 450 |
| Diamond | 50 | 4% | 50 Ã— 4% = 200 |
| **Total** | **1,000** | - | **1,750** |

**Weighted Average Commission = 1,750 / 1,000 = 1.75%**
